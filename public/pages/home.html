Administrator:

The Administrator has full access to manage the website and its users. They can:

  • View a list of all enrolled students in a specific course.
  • View a list of all courses and their details.
  • View a list of all users and their details.
  • Edit and Delete a course's details.
  • Remove a student from a course.

User:

The User has limited access to the website. They can:

  • View a list of all available courses.
  • Enroll in a course.
  • View their enrolled courses.
  • View their profile details.
  • Update their profile details.
